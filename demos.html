SUBMISSIONS OPEN
WANT TO GET ON?
ALENDZI Entertainment is an independent rap label based in Tuzla, Bosnia and Herzegovina. We are currently open to demo submissions. If you make music and you're serious about it, send it through. We listen to everything that comes in.

No industry contacts required. No connections needed. Just send the music. If it's right, we'll be in touch.
SUBMISSION RULES
  • 01
    Standard Audio Quality No low-quality phone recordings. The audio should be clear enough to properly evaluate the music. It doesn't need to be studio-mastered — just audible.
  • 02
    Minimum Length: 1 Minute 30 Seconds The demo must be at least 1:30 long. Short snippets or previews will not be considered. Send a full track.
  • 03
    Original Material Only Do not send covers, remixes, or material you don't own. We only accept original work. No biting.
  • 04
    Email Submission Send your demo to alendzi@protonmail.com with the subject line DEMO – [Your Name]. Include a link to the audio file or attach it directly.
  • 05
    Response Policy We listen to everything. If we're interested, we will respond. If you don't hear back — it's not the right fit this time. Do not send follow-up emails asking for a verdict.

Ready? Send your demo directly to the label.
