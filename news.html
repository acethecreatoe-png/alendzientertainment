Label Officially Renamed ALENDZI Entertainment
12.01.2024 ALENDZI Entertainment
The label has officially adopted its final name: ALENDZI Entertainment. Previously operating as Da House Records, Green Records, and briefly ALENDZI Studios, the Tuzla-based independent label settles on its permanent identity. Current roster: N.T.P., A.L.E.N, Vertex, MC Lukas.

Roster Expands Under ALENDZI Studios Name
11.2024 ALENDZI Entertainment
During the ALENDZI Studios period, N.T.P. and MC Lukas join the label. Founding member BeatzBlaze renames to Vertex; K99 renames to A.L.E.N. The label's roster takes its core shape ahead of the final rebrand.

Da House Records Founded in Tuzla
11.08.2024 ALENDZI Entertainment
Da House Records is founded in Tuzla, Bosnia and Herzegovina — the independent label that would eventually become ALENDZI Entertainment. Founding members K99 and BeatzBlaze (later A.L.E.N and Vertex) establish the label on this date.
